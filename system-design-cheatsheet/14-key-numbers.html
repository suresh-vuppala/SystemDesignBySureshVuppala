Back-of-Envelope Estimation

Quick math for interviews

Storage: 1M users × 1KB = 1GB · 1B users × 1KB = 1TB
Time: 1 day ≈ 100K sec · 1 year ≈ 30M sec
Traffic: 1M DAU × 10 req/day = 10M/day ÷ 100K sec = ~100 RPS avg · Peak = 3-5x
Powers of 2: 2^10=1KB · 2^20=1MB · 2^30=1GB · 2^40=1TB · 2^50=1PB
80/20 Rule: 20% of data = 80% of traffic (cache hot data)
Rule of 72: Doubling time = 72 / growth% (10%/month → doubles in ~7 months)
